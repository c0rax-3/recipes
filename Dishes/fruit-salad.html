🥗 Ingredients
    - 1 cup seedless watermelon (cubed)
    - 1 cup pineapple chunks (fresh or canned)
    - 1 cup green grapes (halved)
    - 1 cup blueberries
    - 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice (optional for a zesty kick)
    👩‍🍳 Steps
    - Prep the fruit: Wash all fruit thoroughly. Cut watermelon and pineapple into bite-sized chunks. Halve the grapes.
    - Combine: In a large bowl, gently mix watermelon, pineapple, grapes, and blueberries.
    - Add lime juice: Drizzle lime juice over the fruit for extra brightness (optional but recommended).
    - Toss lightly: Use a spoon to gently toss everything together so the flavors mingle.
    - Serve or chill: Serve immediately or refrigerate for 30 minutes to enjoy it cool and crisp.
